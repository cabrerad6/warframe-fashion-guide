Excalibur Umbra — Void Wraith

DARK THEME
Primary: #0d0d0d (Abyss Black)
Secondary: #1a0000 (Blood Dark)
Accent: #8b0000 (Deep Crimson)
Tertiary: #2a0a0a (Ember Shade)

⚔️ Armor Recommendations

  • Umbra Armor Set (default — matches silhouette perfectly)
  • Pakal Armor — angular and aggressive
  • Targis Prime Armor — for a regal dark warrior

🌊 Syandana

  • Asa Syandana — sleek, minimal
  • Garuda Talons Syandana — threatening drape

✨ Ephemera

  • Bleeding Body Ephemera or Smoking Body Ephemera (dark smoke)

Keep energy color deep crimson. Use the Stalker palette for true void-blacks. Avoid any white channels — maintain full darkness for this look.
